# ReactStory – Privacy Policy
_Last updated: December 2025_

## 1. Introduction
ReactStory (“the App”, “we”, “our”, “us”) is an interactive visual novel designed to teach programming concepts through story-driven gameplay.  
We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user information.

ReactStory does **not collect, store, share, or transfer** any personal information.

---

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect
ReactStory does **not** collect any of the following:

- Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
- Device identifiers or IP addresses
- Location information
- Analytics or usage data
- Cookies or tracking technologies
- Payment or financial data
- User-generated content
- Data shared externally or stored on cloud services

All gameplay data is stored **locally on your device** and is never transmitted to any server.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services
ReactStory does **not** integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.  
The app does not include:

- Analytics SDKs  
- Advertising networks  
- Tracking tools  
- Cloud save systems  
- Social login services

---

## 4. Children’s Privacy
ReactStory may be used by users aged **9+**.  
We do **not** knowingly collect data from children.

---

## 5. Data Security
Since no personal information is collected or stored, no data is transmitted outside the device.  
Deleting the app removes all locally stored data.

---

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect improvements or legal compliance.  
The latest version will always be available on this page.
